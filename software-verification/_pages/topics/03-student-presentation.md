# Topic 3: Student Presentation

## Overview

Student presentations provide opportunities for peer learning and sharing of research findings, tool experiences, and practical applications of V&V techniques.

## Presentation Topics

Students will present on various aspects of software verification and validation, including:
- Research papers and case studies
- Tool demonstrations and evaluations
- Industry practices and experiences
- Advanced topics and techniques
